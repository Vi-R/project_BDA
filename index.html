Who we are

ANT-Engineering company develops and introduces innovative technologies under the trade mark ANT since 2006. ANT technologies was made by Anton Negulyaev, CEO of the company. ANT technologies are completely safe for the environment and human, that was proved by certificates of safety and scientific research of independent institutes. We are used to build new and repair existing roads.

Application of technologies allows to reduce the cost of construction by more than 30%, to shorten the time of production, to extend the life of roads. Roads built using ANT technologies are operated in all climatic zones, from India to the Arctic Circle.

We help our partners at all stages from the training of technical specialists to the application of ANT technologies before the construction of the first road sections.
