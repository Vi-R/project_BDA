Who we are

ANT-Engineering brings to your attention own innovative ANT soil stabilization technology. This technology is completely different from other world's technologies of soil stabilization for a number of factors.

The ANT technology was first developed for road works in difficult climatic conditions of Russia such as: a large amount of rain and snow, a large number of cycles of freezing and thawing of the road during the winter period, as well as air temperatures from + 40°C to - 50°C.

Now more than 300 km of roads of various technical categories have been constructed in Russia and they are operated in all climatic zones from the southern steppes to Eastern Siberia.

Roads constructed using the ANT technology are also found in Belarus and Moldova. The distinctive feature of ANT soil stabilization technology is the high strength of the stabilized soil, the low construction cost, the ease of application of the technology and 100% safety for nature and humans.

The main element of the technology is the ANT soil stabilizer. It is produced from natural organic raw materials and is completely safe for nature and humans.

ANT stabilizer at the molecular level initiates redoxreaction in the soil, resulting in the formation of new crystalline compounds between individual soil particles, that’s why the soil becomes monolithic.

This reaction repeats the processes of formation of sedimentary rock materials in nature.

ANT stabilizer is a concentrated liquid and, together with water, is used to stabilize the soil. The required amount of water is calculated, taking into account the achievement of optimum moisture when compaction of this soil and its natural moisture. The rate of consumption of ANT stabilizer is 0.007% of the mass of the soil. The stabilizer is mixes with water and added into the soil. In practice, under different climatic conditions and soil moisture, the solubility of the stabilizer with water is from 1: 250 to 1: 1500 (one liter of ANT stabilizer for the quantity of liters of water.
